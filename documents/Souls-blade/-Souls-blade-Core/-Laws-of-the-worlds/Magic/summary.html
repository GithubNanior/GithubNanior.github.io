Magic typically refers to the effects of mana, it may occur naturally or by something's influence.

Human mages

Humans born with magic are pretty rare, and even rarer in ancient times.

Back then, humans born with the ability to use magic were believed to be destined great things by the gods.
Ironically the pressure that the title carries- Or rather the way they were seen led many to hide themselves or their powers.

Trivia

  • It has been a long standing belief that a mere object cannot control mana as it does not have a will of its own.
  • Mage/Magus comes from the word "magic", as they were the first magic users in history.
