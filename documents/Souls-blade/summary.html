About Nanior

Welcome to Souls blade!
This all started as a verbal and gesture game created by me and my brother which we played for many years, called 然后呢, due to how often we say it when playing, like proceeding a cutscene in video games. Although my brother kind of stopped playing, I moved on to hosting games for other people on Discord!
