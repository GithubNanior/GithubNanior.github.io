About the game

I decided to improve some aspects of RogueBlade I, which... Well, to be honest, didn't look too nice... So yeah, here we are! RogueBlade II, much improved compared to it's prequel!
Or not... The prequel got updated soon after with it's own new features, and now it's RB I (Dark, mysterious, difficult) vs RB II (Polished, joyful, relaxed)
P.S I'll add merchants and multiple floors back as coins are not useable here, although how I'll progress also depends on the feedback I get, so please do write a comment for expansions you wish to see, or things you dislike!

Desktop Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move
  • LMB to attack
  • E to interact
  • Esc to pause

Touchscreen Controls

  • Left joystick to move
  • Drag right joystick to aim
  • Release right joystick to attack
  • The button to interact appears when near interactable objects
