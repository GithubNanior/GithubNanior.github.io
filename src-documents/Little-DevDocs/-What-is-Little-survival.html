Overview

Little survival, my blend of fight, explore, survive and loot, somewhere between being a Rogue-like and a survival sandbox, where despite the innocent name, blocky graphics and (fairly) vibrant colors, is actually about hardcore survival in a dangerous world.

  • Each new beginning, each new world could be very different, the things you lack as a beginner is different, so are the biomes and foes!
  • Limited in numbers but miniboss-like enemies, or rather souls-like, they're mostly less easy to fight but you can usually avoid having to face multiple at the same time.
  • Despite being hardcore, players can obtain extra lives with each milestone in the game.
  • There're things you could do that doesn't involve combat! So that you have something to do while waiting things out, or get stronger in a more casual way.
  • Keep in mind that there're also dangers approaching gradually, like the first night, the first winter, and random events (Just so that the player never feel completely safe)
