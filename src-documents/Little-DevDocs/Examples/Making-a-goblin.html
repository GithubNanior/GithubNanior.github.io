Introductions

Creatures are an important part of many games, they pose a challenge, interact with the player and the world, or simply adds a spark of life. So we'll be demonstrating how to make one in Little Survival step by step, specifically, we'll be making a Goblin here.

1. Creating the prefab

Firstly, we need to create a model, in our specific case we simply made a goblin using transforms as pivots and cubes, but you may also make a rigged model.

2. Adding essential components and configuring the prefab

  1. Add the SaveableObject component, as the goblin is considered a SaveableObject.
  2. Add a new MonoBehaviour inheriting from Creature, it will be responsible for most of the creature's behaviours and bindings.
  3. AnimatableCreatureProperties
