Types of magic users

  1. Mages can control Mana by innate means, you can't easily disarm them as their very body is a weapon.
  2. DemiMages only partially innately control mana, some would refer to one who's both a wizard and a mage as a demimage
  3. Wizards are beings who control mana by external means, often a weapon or an accessory.
  4. The rest are just people who use magic items, you don't become a wizard by drinking magic potions nor by using scrolls.

Mediums

Mediums are objects that when in contact with a person, allows a person to sense and control one or more types of mana.
Even though mediums typically refer to weapons and accessories, a mage's soul technically has a medium, too.

Controlling mana

People who control mana relies on their feeling, a sense independent from sight, hearing, or smell.
It is like an aura of their influence, weaking over distance...
This feeling is fuzzy and rarely ever lets you control the whole cloud, but everything that you already control would remain within your control until focus is dropped.

Mana itself typically weights an insignificant amount, making it reasonably easy to control...
However, solidified mana or mana containers are practically never viable to be controlled by any normal being.

Trivia

  • Funnily, blind people are typically better at sensing mana.
  • Few can deflect magic projectiles by grabbing control of it, it's at the same time both a specialitized skill and limited by the mana types you're aligned with.
  • When creating solid projectiles from mana, the projectile's velocity can only be induced while or before it's solidified.
