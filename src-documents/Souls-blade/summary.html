Introductions

Welcome to Souls blade - a text based RPG system developed by Nanior and Od!

Souls blade's content is mostly made for a medieval fantasy setting, and has a highly dynamic combat system, while trying to retain numbered stats and rules.

Nanior's view

To me, this system is an attempt at putting my normal way of hosting games into a numbered system.

My playstyle came from my history of many years of rule-less gameplay with my brother, a game we made and called 然后呢, due to how often we say it when playing, like proceeding a cutscene in video games.


Numbers and rules

This is my personal thought, but... I feel like people rely on rules too often too much, and have difficulty accepting when there're no numbers or rules to hold onto.

My favourite way to host games is to go by the feel, I give the player a sheet with their items, stats relative to average person and so on...
And then use the dice to introduce some randomness, which gives me the ability to keep the game balanced, and spare the players from the worst fates...
It leads to really epic and dynamic combat too!

But okay, if people insist on playing with strict rules, that's what this system was made for.
