Me as a programmer

I've actually been programming games in Unity since many, many years by now. Since at least the age of 10, I think?
Although in the foremost years I wasn't taking it seriously at all, but I gradually started taking it more seriously as I grew older.
Recent years though, besides game development I also picked up some Web development, so I've learned HTML, CSS, JS and wrote this website, also have a bit of experience with Flask, bootstrap and tailwind.
Along the way, I also have experience making games on a graphics calculator, with barebones C#, have some experience with C, C++, Python and other stuff.

Although I must admit, my liking for game development is... A little weird...
I find myself enjoying designing and making the game's framework, adding few pieces of content to test the design, but struggling to push myself to actually add content and polishing for gameplay.
So... I guess I can't actually be an indie developer, but need to find my place in a team instead, as I can't do it all alone.
